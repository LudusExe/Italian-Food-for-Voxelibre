This mod adds a variety of traditional Italian foods, ingredients, and cooking tools to Voxelibre and Mineclonia, enhancing survival gameplay with new farming, crafting, and potion mechanics. Grow fresh tomatoes and basil, craft delicious meals, and enjoy new gameplay mechanics like potion effects. Perfect for players who love farming, cooking, or adding some cultural flavor to their game.

### Features:

* **Foods**: Pizza, Lasagna, Spaghetti, Bruschetta (with multiple toppings), Cannoli, Tiramisu and more.
* **Ingredients**: Basil, Tomatoes, Mozzarella, Sheep Cheese, Sauces (Tomato, Pesto), Dough, and fresh Olive Oil.
* **Farming**: Multi-stage crops for Tomatoes, Basil, and Olive Trees.
* **Crafting**: Use authentic recipes to prepare complex dishes.
* **Tools**: Wooden and Iron Rolling Pins, Pizza Cutter Wheel, and the new **Olive Wood** for crafting.
* **Potions**: Coffee-based **Sugar Coffee Potion** with speed, jump, and dig boosts.
* **Achievements**: Earn rewards for eating Italian specialties like Pizza and Lasagna.
* **Structures**: Discover Pizzerias scattered throughout the world, each containing loot.
* **Music**: A new music disc titled "Tarantella" available via the jukebox.
